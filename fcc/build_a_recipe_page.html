Pasta Recipe

A simple pasta recipe to make homemade pasta with just some flour and eggs, easily scapable depending on how much you may need

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cutting board
  • Rolling pin
  • Knife

Instructions

  1. Make the flour in a bowl on the cutting board
  2. Add the egg to the center of the flour
  3. Combine egg and flour until uniformed dough
  4. Roll out til thing with a rolling pin
  5. Cut into desired length with a knife
  6. Boil until cooked
